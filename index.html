Survival Quiz

Zombie Apocalypse

We were presented with a challenge, we needed to find a way to see which friends were most likely to survive the zombie apocalypse.

Goal

Create a way for people to understand their likelyhood of survivability during a zombie apocalypse.

Solution

This app calculates weighted scores for the user which displays their likelihood in the end to survive a zombie apocalypse. The app helps users see how far along the evaluation process they are with subtle micro-interactions which effortlessly guide their next steps.

Result

Upon finishing the quiz users can see their survivability score.

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Git

Dealer Admin Panel

This is a backoffice application designed to help office managers keep track of sales agents. The api endpoint sends data to other marketing and sales tools allowing the office manager to keep track of sales agents from a single point.

Goal

Create a single source of truth for managing dealer sales agents

Solution

This app allows office managers to create edit and delete sales agents from a database.

Result

This app saves office managers time and eliminates the need to re-duplicate the effort to maintain a list of sales agents across multiple sales and marketing tools

The Farmers Bazaar

We started the Farmers Bazaar for people living during the pandemic. It's challenging for the immunocompromised to get fresh produce from farmers' markets without exposing the risk of exposure. We wanted to give people a way not to feel panicked and anxious every time they want to get fresh farmers' produce.

Goal

Create a way to connect farmers to local shoppers looking for produce.

Solution

This app allows farmers to upload inventory and connect that inventory to local shoppers.

Result

Eliminate the risk of Covid19 exposure for both farmers and shoppers to access fresh farm vegetables.
